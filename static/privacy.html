Privacy Policy

App: Rev & Learn
Developer: Martin Wambugu
Last updated: 28 March 2026

1. Overview

Rev & Learn is an educational racing game designed for children aged 3 and above. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

2. Data We Collect

Rev & Learn does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal details to play.

All game progress (coins, upgrades, unlocked content) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

3. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no data at all, the app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

4. Third-Party Services

Rev & Learn does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media services. No data is shared with any third party.

5. Internet Access

The app does not require or use an internet connection during gameplay. No data is transmitted over the internet.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
